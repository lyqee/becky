my values

At the core of everything I do is a profound sense of compassion. I believe in empathy and kindness as the driving forces behind meaningful change. Understanding the struggles of others and responding with genuine care is essential to my personal and professional life.

my hobbies

I love to draw and doodle. Here is one of my sketches. I especially love drawing characters and animals, because there are so many different ways to express them. Drawing calms me down and keeps my mind occupied, another reason why I love to draw. I was actually interested in pursuing art, however I got really burnt out from my art exam in secondary school. Art is the most fun when there aren't rules to follow. So I figured it is best to leave art as my hobby instead. Creating my own website was really fun for me, although it took me quite some time to learn everything. From thinking about how I want my site to look, to successfully executing it and making it look good made this project really exciting. Combining both creativity and coding made me more happy to do this assessment, rather than the usual dread. Other than drawing, I have other art related hobbies. Such as crocheting, needle felting, painting, and I'll be learning how to knit and sew during the holidays. Pottery is also another hobby I want to pursue, but given the amount of free time I have, it is not possible for now.
